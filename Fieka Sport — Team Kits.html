Who We Are

Outfitting Teams.
Elevating Identity.

Fieka Sport is a custom kit manufacturer built for coaches, clubs, and organisations who refuse to settle. We design and produce fully sublimated performance kits — jerseys, shorts, tracksuits, training wear — tailored to your team's identity from the ground up.

We don't do off-the-shelf. Every order is made to spec, with your colours, your badge, your name. We handle the design, the production, and the delivery — so you can focus on the game.

Every kit we produce is engineered for the demands of real competition. We use performance fabrics and full-bleed sublimation printing so the colour never fades, the fit never fails, and the look always commands respect.
